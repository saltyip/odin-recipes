description

Chicken teriyaki is a savory-sweet Japanese-inspired dish featuring tender chicken coated in a glossy, flavorful sauce made from soy sauce, mirin, sugar, and other seasonings.

It's a popular weeknight meal known for its quick preparation and rich taste

ingredients

  • Boneless, skinless chicken thighs
  • Soy sauce
  • Mirin
  • Water
  • Fresh ginger
  • Sesame seeds
  • Scallions

steps

  2. In a medium bowl, combine soy sauce, mirin, brown sugar, honey, water, garlic, ginger, and cornstarch. Whisk until smooth and no lumps remain.
