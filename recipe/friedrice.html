fried rice

index

description

Fried rice is a versatile and popular dish made by stir-frying cooked rice with a variety of ingredients such as eggs, vegetables, meat, or seafood, often seasoned with soy sauce, sesame oil, and aromatics like garlic and ginge

ingredients

  • Cooked white rice
  • Large eggs
  • Onion
  • Scallions
  • Soy sauce
  • Kosher salt and ground white pepper
  • Toasted sesame oil

steps

  1. If using freshly cooked rice, spread it on a baking sheet and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to dry out and cool completely; this prevents mushiness
  2. Heat 1/2 tablespoon (7ml) of oil in a wok over high heat until smoking. Add half of the rice and cook, stirring and tossing, until pale brown and toasted with a lightly chewy texture, about 3 minutes. Transfer to a bowl. Repeat with another 1/2 tablespoon oil and the remaining rice.
  3. Return the rice to the wok and press it up the sides, leaving a space in the middle. Add 1/2 tablespoon (7ml) oil to the space. Add onion, carrot, scallions, and garlic, and cook, stirring gently, until lightly softened and fragrant, about 1 minute.
  4. Push the rice to the side of the wok and add the remaining 1/2 tablespoon (7ml) oil. Break the eggs into the oil and season with a little salt
  5. Add frozen peas and continue to toss and stir until the peas are thawed and every grain of rice is separate
